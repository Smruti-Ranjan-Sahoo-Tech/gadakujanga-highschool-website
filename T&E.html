Terms and Conditions

1. Introduction

Welcome to Gadakujanga Govt. UGHC , a recognized high school under the Government of Odisha HSC Board. By accessing and using our services, you agree to abide by these terms and conditions.

2. Admission and Enrollment

  • Admission is granted based on eligibility criteria set by the Odisha HSC Board.
  • Students must submit valid documents, including birth certificates, previous academic records, and identity proofs.
  • Fees must be paid as per the school’s guidelines, and late payments may incur penalties.

3. Academic Policies

  • Students must adhere to the syllabus and curriculum prescribed by the Odisha HSC Board.
  • Attendance of at least 75% is mandatory to appear for board examinations.
  • Academic dishonesty, including cheating and plagiarism, will lead to disciplinary action.

4. Code of Conduct

  • Students must maintain discipline within the school premises.
  • Any form of harassment, bullying, or violence is strictly prohibited.
  • Use of mobile phones during school hours without permission is not allowed.

5. Examination and Evaluation

  • Examinations will be conducted as per the schedule provided by the Odisha HSC Board.
  • Internal assessments, practicals, and projects contribute to final grading.
  • Any malpractice during examinations may lead to disqualification.

6. Fees and Refund Policy

  • All fees must be paid within the stipulated time.
  • Fees once paid are non-refundable, except in special cases approved by the management.
  • Additional charges may apply for extracurricular activities and special programs.

7. Use of Facilities

  • Students must use school facilities responsibly, including classrooms, laboratories, and libraries.
  • Any damage to school property must be compensated as per the school’s policies.

8. Parent and Guardian Responsibilities

  • Parents must ensure their child follows school rules and regulations.
  • Any changes in student details, such as address or emergency contact, must be updated promptly.
  • Regular meetings between parents and teachers will be conducted for academic progress discussions.

9. Privacy and Data Protection

Personal information of students and staff will be kept confidential and used only for academic and administrative purposes. The school follows government regulations on data protection and security.

10. Amendments to Terms

The school reserves the right to modify these terms and conditions at any time. Parents and students will be notified of significant changes through official communication channels.
